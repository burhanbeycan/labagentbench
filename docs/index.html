About

The Cooper Group advert highlights human‑ and literature‑informed discovery and hybrid LLM‑Bayesian optimisation. This demo illustrates the core idea: text hints become priors/constraints that guide exploration in a black‑box search space.

  • Vanilla BO: GP surrogate + LCB acquisition.
  • Text‑prior BO: sample candidates inside hint‑derived bounds (or near a hint centre) to speed convergence.
  • Research extension: replace the hint parser with an LLM/RAG component that extracts constraints from papers, lab notes, or ELNs.
